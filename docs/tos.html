ReelForge — Last updated: March 2026

1. Overview

ReelForge is an internal content management tool used by its operator to draft and schedule BookTok video content to a single TikTok creator account. It is not a public-facing consumer product.

2. Use of TikTok Integration

ReelForge uses the TikTok Content Posting API solely to upload video drafts to an account owned and operated by the tool's administrator. No third-party TikTok accounts are accessed or managed through this tool.

3. Data Collection

ReelForge does not collect, store, or share personal data from any TikTok user other than the administrator account used to operate the tool. OAuth tokens obtained from TikTok are stored securely and used only to perform draft uploads.

ReelForge is provided as-is for internal use. The operator makes no warranties and accepts no liability for interruptions to service or changes to the TikTok API.
